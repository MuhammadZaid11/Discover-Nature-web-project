Why Nature Matters

Biodiversity is the foundation of human existence. From the oxygen we breathe to the water we drink and the food we eat, healthy ecosystems provide indispensable services. When we protect nature, we protect our own future.

Planting Trees

A single mature tree can absorb nearly 48 pounds of carbon dioxide per year and release enough oxygen to support two human beings.

The Power of Recycling

Recycling just one aluminum can saves enough energy to run a television for three hours. It reduces landfill waste and conserves raw materials.

×

Water Conservation

Turning off the tap while brushing your teeth can save up to 8 gallons of water a day. That's nearly 3,000 gallons a year!
